Taylor swift

Her full name is Taylor Alison Swift and comes from an upper middle class from Wyomissing.

She was born on 13. december 1989 in Reading (Pennylvania). Shes the most famous female pop singer and can also play the guitar.

Her most famous song is „Shake it off“ which got released in 2014. She got 50 awards and 40 trophies since 2007, that makes her the artist with the most awards at this time.

Backstory:

In 2004 she signed her first songwriting-contract with Sony/ATV Music Publishing. The year after she got her first record with Big Machine Records. She released her first album in 2006 which is named after her singer name. The next two albums were „Fearless“ and „Speak now“ which came out in 2008 and 2010. The two songs „Love story“ and „You belong with me“ were the first songs that stormed the charts. The next album got released in 2017 named „Reputation“ with the number-one-single „Look what you made me do“. In 2018 she left Big Machine Records after a big fight and went to Republic Records instead. One year after she released her 7. album „Lover“. While the Corona-19-pandemic she brought out the albums „Folklore“ and „Evermore“ with the lead-singles „Cardigan“ and „Willow“ which were the top songs of the Hot 100. Abit later she redid the song „All too well“ (10:13min) which was the longest number-1-hit from the Hot 100. In 2022 she released her last album till now which is „Midnights“. It was the 5th album which got over million sells and broke streaming records. The album has the song „Anti-Hero“ which is another number-1-hit.

Our favourite album is 1989. She released the album on the 27. Oktober 2014. It has 19 songs. The album cover shows a poloroid picture of Tylor. Shake it of ist the most popular song but our favourits are: style, wildest dreams, new romantics, this love and out off the woods.
